EXTRAORDINARY
MEASURES

WE ARE STRINGENTLY ADHERING TO ALL HEALTH AND SAFETY PROTOCOLS FOR THE WELLBEING OF OUR GUESTS AND EMPLOYEES WITHOUT COMPROMISING THE QUALITY OF ENTERTAINMENT. ENTIRE CINEMA IS SANITISED USING ADVANCED ULV ELECTROSTATIC SPRAY GUNS WITH CERTIFIED LONG LASTING PROTECTION. INDOOR AIR QUALITY IN OUR AUDITORIUMS IS IMPROVED WITH INCREASED INTAKE OF FRESH AIR AND MAINTENANCE OF TEMPERATURE AND HUMIDITY LEVELS AS PER GOVT. DIRECTIVES. ALL HIGH TOUCH POINTS ARE RELIGIOUSLY DISINFECTED AT REGULAR INTERVALS AND AUDITORIUMS ARE DISINFECTED BEFORE THE START OF EACH SHOW. FOOD PACKAGING, CROCKERY AND CUTLERY WILL BE STERILIZED IN UV CABINETS BEFORE SERVING, WHILE ONLY DISPOSABLE FOOD PACKAGING WILL BE USED IN REGULAR CINEMAS.

SOCIAL DISTANCING

LAYOUTS FOR SEATING AND GUEST MOVEMENT HAVE BEEN ALTERED TO ENABLE PHYSICAL DISTANCING IN AUDITORIUMS, LOBBIES, ELEVATORS, BOX OFFICE, CONCESSIONS AND COMMON AREAS. FLOOR MARKERS ARE PLACED AT ALL QUEUING POINTS, STARTING FROM THE BOX OFFICE TILL CINEMA EXIT.

WE HAVE MODIFIED OUR PROCEDURES USING TECHNOLOGY FOR SEAMLESS, YET PERSONALIZED SERVICE. WITH DIGITAL PAYMENTS AND NON-INVASIVE THERMAL SCREENING, THE TOUCH OF CARE REPLACES THE PHYSICAL ONE.
